Dominik Krzemiński

_______________________________

I'm a computational neuroscience PhD student working at the Cognitive and Computational Brain lab based at the Cardiff University Brain Research Imaging Centre. Moreover, I help Appsilon Data Science team to develop their open source R packages. I'm interested in neuroscience and machine-learning, particularly decision making (of humans and machines). I'm also advocate of the open science movement and enjoy contributing to open source tools.
